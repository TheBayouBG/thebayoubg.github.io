The Bayou Bar & Grill

The menu offered at the Bayou has a variety of dishes aimed to please every customer. Starting with appetizers, we have everything from whole crawfish to fried green beans to a Gator Creator sampler platter. All sandwiches are served po'boy style; on toasted hoagie buns. These could include the Bayou burgers which are hand pattied daily with fresh ground beef to blackened chicken or pulled pork. A more unusual dish is the frog legs and ranks in popularity with the fried catfish dinner. Some more authentic dishes include gumbo and red beans & rice. We also offer a variety of desserts to finish off your meal.

About

The Bayou Bar & Grill LLC was established in October 2005 by Bill and Linda Wooten. The Cajun themed restaurant had been the life long dream of Bill who lived in Louisiana for many years and developed a love for Cajun food.

Today his dream continues as Linda and their daughter Jenny strive to offer a pleasurable dining experience with great, quality food, and a good atmosphere at a reasonable price.
